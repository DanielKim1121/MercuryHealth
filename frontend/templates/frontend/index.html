Our Device

Keeps your loved ones safe.

The small, unintrusive camera of our device can detect elderly falls and other human actions in real time. Emergency contacts or services will be messaged instantly if anything is wrong.

Encourages independence.

Unlike current technologies which use wearable technology (like the Apple Watch™ and Life Alert) to analyze a person’s actions, our HeraHome device easily installs in any desired room and monitors entirely from a distance.

Personalizes care.

Videos captured by our device are sent to our servers, which anonymously analyze the data to look for patterns in a person's movement.

How it Works

INSTALL
Our team will install the device in a desired room.

REGISTER
Create a login and register your device to receive notifications.

CAPTURE + IDENTIFY
Videos captured by device will analyze patterns in movement.

ALERT
Emergency contact/services will be notified when a fall is detected.
